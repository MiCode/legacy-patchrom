USB tethering

Your computer can share your device's Internet connection if you tether them together with USB cable
  • Tethering works with Windows 7, Windows Vista, and Linux

  • You cannot connect SD card to your computer when tethered

  • For more information, including how to use USB tethering with other operating systems, go to http://www.android.com/tether#usb

Portable WLAN hotspot

You can turn your device into portable WLAN hotsopt, to share its Internet connection with computers or other devices
  • Unable to use device applications to access Internet via WLAN connection while device serves as portable WLAN hotspot

  • You can configure portable WLAN hotspot in WLAN hotsopt settings

  • For more information, visit http://www.android.com/tether#wifi
